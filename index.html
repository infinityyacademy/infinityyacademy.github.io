Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our academy.

What is the teaching methodology at Infinity Academy?

Our methodology focuses on conceptual clarity, interactive sessions, and regular practice. We use a blend of traditional teaching and modern tools to make learning engaging and effective. Our small class sizes ensure every student gets individual attention.

How do you track a student's progress?

We conduct weekly and monthly assessments to evaluate student progress. Detailed performance reports are shared with both students and parents. Our faculty provides personalized feedback and guidance based on these reports to help students improve.

What kind of doubt-clearing support is available?

We offer dedicated doubt-clearing sessions after every class. Students can also schedule one-on-one sessions with their teachers for complex topics. Our goal is to ensure no student leaves a class with an unresolved question.

What are the class timings?

Class timings vary by subject and batch. We offer both morning and evening batches to accommodate different student schedules. Please contact us for the detailed schedule for the course you are interested in.

Do you provide demo classes?

Yes, we provide 2 free demo classes so that students can experience our teaching style and methodology before enrolling in a course.

Are classes available online or offline?

We currently provide only offline classes.

What are the fees and payment options?

Our fees vary by course and batch. Flexible payment options are available, including monthly installments. Please contact our team for detailed fee structure.
